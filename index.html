Privacy Policy for Hidayah

Last updated: 2026-04-14

Hidayah respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

1. Information We Collect

Hidayah does not collect or store personal data on external servers.

The app may access location data only to calculate accurate prayer times based on your location.

2. How We Use Information

Location data is used solely to provide accurate prayer times and improve app functionality.

3. Notifications

If enabled, the app may send prayer reminders and important app-related updates. Notifications are handled locally on your device.

4. Data Sharing

Hidayah does not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties. The app does not track users across apps or websites.

5. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. No personal data is stored externally.

6. Third-Party Services

Hidayah does not use third-party analytics or advertising services that track users.

7. Your Consent

By using Hidayah, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
